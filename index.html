SUSS Shuttle Booking (Prototype)

Prototype for an autonomous bus shuttle from MRT interchanges to SUSS before class. Focus is on reducing stress and travel time on the way to campus.

1. Student details

Anti-abuse rule: only one active booking per student ID. Repeated no-shows (3+) will block further bookings.

2. Choose shuttle

Capacity: 40 seats total, 0 taken, 40 remaining.

The app shows capacity so students can choose a less crowded bus. Once full, no more bookings are allowed so everyone sits.

3. Your booking & QR code

After booking, your active booking will appear here with a generated QR code. In the real system, you would scan this at the bus door to board.

Bus amenities (concept)

  • Amenities kit: shared cushions, tissues
  • Shades / dimming windows or curtains
  • Digital clock for time awareness
  • Free Wi-Fi for study on the go
  • Complimentary bottled water and light snacks
  • Overhead compartment for bulky bags
  • Foldable tables (airplane-style)
  • USB charging ports at every seat

These features support comfort and stress reduction: students can rest, study, and arrive at SUSS in a good mood.

Anti-abuse & fairness features (prototype logic)

  • Only SUSS IDs can book (in real app, verified with SUSS login).
  • One active booking per student ID at any time.
  • Seats are limited to 40 per bus; no standing allowed.
  • Booking opens 2 days before the trip at 9pm and closes once full (conceptual, described here).
  • No-show counter: if you don’t check in at the bus, you get one no-show.
  • 3 no-shows = temporary block from booking. You must contact admin to reset.

This ensures students cannot spam-book seats and prevents non-SUSS students from boarding.
