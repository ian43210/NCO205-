⚖️Fairness & anti-abuse rules

  • Only SUSS IDs can book (real app would use SUSS login).
  • One active booking per student ID at any time.
  • Seats limited to 40 per bus; once full, no more bookings for that trip.
  • Booking opens 2 days before each trip at 9pm and closes when full (concept).
  • No-show: if you do not check in at the bus, your booking is marked “missed”.
  • After 3 no-shows, booking is temporarily blocked until admin review.

🎯Why this shuttle exists

Isabelle, a SUSS student, needs a direct shuttle that is time-efficient, comfortable, and stress-free so she can arrive for classes on time and in a good mood.

  • Routes from Jurong East, Bukit Panjang and East-side MRTs reduce transfers and crowding at Clementi / King Albert Park.
  • Shuttle timings align to SUSS class slots (08:30, 12:00, 15:30, 19:00).
  • Seat-only capacity (40 per bus) ensures no standing and better comfort.

✨Bus amenities (comfort features)

  • Amenities kit: shared cushions and tissues.
  • Shades or curtains to reduce glare and eye strain.
  • Digital clock visible to driver and students.
  • Free Wi-Fi for studying on the go.
  • Complimentary bottled water and light snacks.
  • Overhead compartments for bulky bags.
  • Foldable tables for laptops and notes.
  • USB charging ports beside seats.
