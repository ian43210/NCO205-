📑 My trips (demo explanation)

In a full version, this tab would show your upcoming and past trips:

  • Upcoming trips: your next booked shuttle, with QR code and “Add to calendar”.
  • Past trips: history of rides taken in the last 30 days.
  • Missed trips: rides you booked but did not check in (counted as no-shows).

For this prototype, the logic is simplified and only tracks one active booking in the “Book Shuttle” tab, but this tab explains how the data would be organised.

⚖️ Fairness & anti-abuse rules

  • Only SUSS IDs can book (in real app, verified with SUSS login).
  • One active booking per student ID at any time (prevents blocking multiple buses).
  • Seats limited to 40 per bus; once full, further bookings are blocked.
  • Bookings open 2 days before each trip at 9pm and close when full (concept).
  • No-show: if you do not check in at the bus, your booking is marked “missed”.
  • After 3 no-shows, your account is temporarily blocked for new bookings.

These rules make the shuttle fair, predictable, and prevent students from other schools from abusing the system.

🎯 Why this shuttle exists

Isabelle, a SUSS student, needs a direct shuttle that is time-efficient, comfortable, and stress-free so she can arrive for classes on time and in a good mood.

  • Direct routes from selected MRT interchanges to SUSS reduce line changes and bottlenecks at busy stations like Clementi and King Albert Park.
  • Fixed timings aligned to common SUSS class slots (08:30, 12:00, 15:30, 19:00).
  • Capacity control ensures everyone has a seat; no standing allowed.

✨ Bus amenities (comfort features)

  • Amenities kit: shared cushions and tissues placed near seats.
  • Shades or dimming windows / curtains to reduce glare and eye strain.
  • Digital clock visible to both driver and students.
  • Free Wi-Fi for students who want to study or do work.
  • Complimentary bottled water and light snacks near the entrance.
  • Overhead compartments for bulky bags, freeing legroom.
  • Foldable tables (airplane-style) for laptops and notes.
  • USB charging ports beside seats for devices.

These features support the user need: students can rest, work, and arrive at SUSS less stressed and more prepared for class.
